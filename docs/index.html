Zen Focus

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

Zen Focus ("the App") is a minimalist Pomodoro-style focus timer developed by Ali Imran. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

Zen Focus does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App operates entirely offline.

What the App Stores Locally

The App stores the following data only on your device using Android's SharedPreferences:

  • Timer settings: Focus duration, break duration, theme preference, sound preference
  • Session statistics: Daily focus time, completed sessions count, session history (last 100 sessions)
  • Timer state: Active timer end time (for resuming after app restart)

This data:

  • ✕ Is NOT sent to any server
  • ✕ Is NOT shared with third parties
  • ✕ Is NOT used for advertising or analytics
  • ✓ Stays entirely on your device
  • ✓ Is deleted when you uninstall the App

    • Permissions

    The App requests zero permissions. It does not access:

    • Internet or network
    • Camera or microphone
    • Location
    • Contacts
    • Storage (beyond its own app-scoped storage)
    • Phone state
    • Any other sensitive data

    Third-Party Services

    Zen Focus does NOT use any third-party services such as:

    • Google Analytics
    • Firebase
    • Advertising SDKs
    • Crash reporting tools
    • Social media integrations

    Children's Privacy

    The App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for users of all ages.
